# Legal & Policies

Manage the legal content shown on the FHC website (terms of use, privacy policy, etc.).

---

## Important: This is a Single-Type Page

::: warning One entry only
Legal & Policies has **a single entry**. Editing and publishing it immediately updates the content visible to the public. Always review carefully before publishing.
:::

---

## Editing Legal Content

1. Go to **Content Manager** → **Legal Page**.
2. The single entry opens automatically.
3. Update the **Title** and edit the **Content** using the rich text editor.
4. Click **Save**, then **Publish**.

---

## Field Reference

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Title** | Page heading (e.g., "Terms of Use") |
| **Content** | Full legal text — supports rich text (headings, lists, links) |

---

## Best Practices

| ✅ Do | ❌ Don't |
|------|---------|
| Have legal text reviewed before publishing | Publish unreviewed legal content |
| Use clear headings to structure the document | Write a single block of unformatted text |
| Save a draft and review on website before publishing | Make direct edits and publish without review |
