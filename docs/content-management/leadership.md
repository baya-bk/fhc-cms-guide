# Leadership Team Management

Manage profiles for the Board of Directors and Executive Management team.

---

## Creating or Updating a Leadership Profile

1. Go to **Content Manager** → **Leadership Team**.
2. Click **+ Create new entry** (or select an existing member to edit).
3. Enter the **Name** and **Title** (position name).
4. Upload a **Photo**.
5. Click **Save**, then **Publish**.

---

## Field Reference

| Field | Translated? | Notes |
|-------|-------------|-------|
| **Name** | ✅ Translate | Full name — transliterate into Amharic/Oromo script |
| **Title** | ✅ Translate | Official position title |
| **Photo** | ❌ Shared | One profile photo used in all languages |

---

## Photo Guidelines

::: tip Recommended photo specifications
- **Format**: JPEG or PNG
- **Dimensions**: Square crop (1:1 ratio), minimum 400 × 400 px
- **Style**: Professional portrait, plain or neutral background
- **File size**: Under 2 MB (compress with [TinyPNG](https://tinypng.com) if needed)
:::

---

## Best Practices

| ✅ Do | ❌ Don't |
|------|---------|
| Translate the name and title into Amharic and Oromo | Leave translations as English copies |
| Use a professional, uniform-style portrait | Use casual or low-resolution photos |
| Keep titles consistent with official designations | Abbreviate or improvise job titles |
