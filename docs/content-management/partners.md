# Partners Management

Manage FHC's institutional and development partners shown on the website.

---

## Adding a Partner

1. Go to **Content Manager** → **Partner**.
2. Click **+ Create new entry**.
3. Enter the partner **Name** and **Website Link**.
4. Upload the partner **Logo**.
5. Click **Save**, then **Publish**.

---

## Logo Guidelines

::: tip Recommended logo specifications
- **Format**: PNG with transparent background (preferred) or SVG
- **Dimensions**: Landscape orientation, minimum 200 px wide
- **Background**: Transparent — avoid white boxes around the logo
- **File size**: Under 500 KB
:::

---

## Website Link

Enter the full URL including `https://` (e.g., `https://www.partnerorganization.org`).

::: warning
Always verify the partner's website link is correct and working before publishing.
:::

---

## Best Practices

| ✅ Do | ❌ Don't |
|------|---------|
| Use PNG logos with transparent backgrounds | Use logos with white or colored backgrounds |
| Enter the official partner website URL | Leave the website link blank |
| Keep partner logos consistent in resolution | Mix high and low resolution logos |
