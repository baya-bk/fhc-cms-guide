# Login to the Content Manager

Access the FHC Content Manager to manage all website content.

---

## CMS Address

| Environment | URL |
|-------------|-----|
| **Production** | [https://www.fhc.gov.et:1337/admin](https://www.fhc.gov.et:1337/admin) |

Open the URL in your browser. You should see the login screen.

---

## Steps

1. Enter your **email address**
2. Enter your **password**
3. Click **Login**

You will be taken to the Content Manager dashboard.

## Common Issues

### Forgot your password?
Click **Forgot your password?** on the login screen. A reset link will be sent to your email address.

### Incorrect password
Double-check your email and password. Passwords are case-sensitive. If the issue persists, ask your administrator to reset your account.

### Cannot access the CMS
Ensure you are connected to the internet. If the page does not load, contact the technical team to verify the server is running.

::: warning Access Restriction
Only users with an active account can log in. Contact your administrator to request access.
:::
