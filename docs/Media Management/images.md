# Uploading Images

The Media Library stores all images and graphics used on the FHC website. Images uploaded here can be reused across different content types.

---

## How to Upload an Image

1. Click **Media Library** in the left sidebar.
2. Click **+ Add new assets**.
3. Drag and drop your image files, or click to browse your computer.
4. After uploading, add **Alt Text** describing the image.
5. Click **Finish**.

---

## Before You Upload

::: tip Compress images first
Large images slow down the website. Use [TinyPNG](https://tinypng.com) or [Squoosh](https://squoosh.app) to compress images before uploading.
:::

| Recommendation | Guideline |
|---------------|-----------|
| **Format** | JPEG for photos, PNG for logos/icons with transparency |
| **Cover images** | 1200 × 630 px, 16:9 aspect ratio |
| **Portrait photos** | 400 × 400 px minimum, square (1:1) |
| **Max file size** | Under 2 MB after compression |

---

## File Naming

Use lowercase letters and hyphens — no spaces or special characters:

| ✅ Good | ❌ Bad |
|--------|-------|
| `fhc-headquarters-2025.jpg` | `FHC Headquarters (2025).jpg` |
| `project-addis-ababa-view.jpg` | `project photo & view #1.jpg` |
| `ceo-portrait.jpg` | `CEO Portrait FINAL v2.jpg` |

---

## Organising the Library

- Use the **folder system** to group images by content type or year (e.g., `News/2025`, `Projects/Bole`).
- Always fill in the **Alt Text** field — this improves accessibility and search visibility.

---

## Alt Text Guidelines

| Type of Image | Example Alt Text |
|---------------|-----------------|
| News article photo | `FHC housing project groundbreaking ceremony, Addis Ababa` |
| Leadership portrait | `Director General Abebe Bekele, FHC` |
| Project image | `Bole Housing Complex, phase 2 construction progress` |
